Description

This project is being undertaken as part of a research study conducted by Professor Peter Bruza and Ms. Lauren Fell of QUT.

 

The purpose of this project is to understand how people come to trust whether images and associated text relate to the same situation or event when there may be uncertainties involved regarding both the image and/or the text.when they are using intuition.

 

You are invited to participate in this project because you are a current user of Mechanical Turk.Prolific

 

Participation

Participation will involve looking at 60 combinations of an image paired with text. Each of these pairs will either have 1 or 2 questions attached to them, which you will be asked to answer in Yes/No format. In some cases you will be asked to explain your answers. This will take approximately 15-20 minutes of your time.four images and answering on or two questions about each, as well as providing a gauge of your confidence in making the decision and reasoning on why you made the decision you made.

 

Questions will may include one or two of the following list:

Do you trust that the image does correspond to the situation described by the text?feel a sense of trust?

Do you think the image is potentially fake or has been manipulatedfeel that the person in this image is attractive?

Do you get the sense that this image has been photoshopped?trust the text to be credible?

Do you trust that the text corresponds to the situation depicted in the image?

 

Your participation in this project is entirely voluntary. If you agree to participate you do not have to complete any question(s) you are uncomfortable answering. Your decision to participate or not participate will in no way impact upon your current or future relationship with this requester on Mechanical Turk. If you do agree to participate you can withdraw from the project during your participation without comment or penalty. However, as the survey is anonymous once it has been submitted it will not be possible to withdraw.
